Project Overview

The Smart Hostel Room Allocation System is a web-based platform that automates the entire hostel management process. It replaces manual allocation using registers and spreadsheets with a digital solution where students can apply for rooms, administrators can assign rooms, track occupancy, handle complaints, and manage fees.

This system improves transparency, reduces administrative workload, and ensures efficient hostel operations.


---

Objectives

Automate hostel room allocation.

Maintain a digital record of students, rooms, and availability.

Provide a portal for students to apply, check status, and raise complaints.

Enable admin to assign rooms based on availability, preferences, and eligibility.

Manage hostel fees and generate receipts.

Improve overall hostel communication and monitoring.



---

Proposed System Features

1. Student Module

User registration & login.

Apply for hostel room.

View available rooms and occupancy status.

Receive room confirmation through dashboard.

Pay hostel fees online (optional add-on).

Lodge complaints (electricity, water, cleaning, etc.).

Track status of submitted complaints.


2. Admin Module

Admin login.

Manage hostel blocks, floors, and rooms.

View real-time room availability.

Auto or manual room allocation.

Approve/reject student applications.

Manage student check-in & check-out.

Maintain fee records & generate reports.

Assign staff to handle complaints.
